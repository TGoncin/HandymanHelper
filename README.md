<h2 align="center" id="project-abstract">Project Abstract</h2>
<br>
Finding quality home services is becoming increasingly difficult as the communication gap between contractors and clients widens. Current platforms often prioritize ad revenue over service quality, forcing skilled professionals to compete for "visibility" rather than demonstrating their craftsmanship. This creates a lack of trust and leaves both parties frustrated with sub-optimal results.
<br>
<br>
<strong>Handyman Helper</strong> is a digital marketplace designed to restore transparency to the trades. By connecting local professionals directly with clients, our platform ensures quality work at a fair, verified price. Built on modern marketplace frameworks, it introduces specialized verification and bidding tools tailored specifically for the contracting industry.
<br>
<br>
Our vision is to remove the "advertising hamster wheel" and eliminate dishonest middlemen, allowing workers to focus on their craftsmanship and clients to get the best value for their dollar.
<br>
<br>
While the demand for skilled help is at an all-time high, existing platforms continue to fail users. <strong>Handyman Helper</strong> solves this by providing:
<br>
<br>
<ul style="margin-left: 40px;">
  <li><strong>Zero-Cost Listings</strong><br>
    &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Removing the pay-to-play barriers that force contractors into expensive, sub-optimal payment plans just to stay visible.
  </li>
  <li><strong>Two-Way Verified Ratings</strong><br>
    &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;A secure system where reviews are only unlocked after a deal is struck, preventing bot manipulation and ensuring accountability for both parties.
  </li>
  <li><strong>Market Fairness & Transparency</strong><br>
    &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Reducing high overhead for contractors and providing clients with an easy way to compare quality and credentials beyond major corporations.
  </li>
  <li><strong>Direct Bidding System</strong><br>
    &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Bringing the efficiency of large-scale government bidding to local home projects, ensuring the most competitive pricing for every job.
  </li>
</ul>
<br>
By integrating verified credentials and a documented project history, Handyman Helper creates an ecosystem built on credibility. We aren't just building a marketplace; we're contributing to local economic growth by simplifying how trustworthy, affordable home solutions are found and delivered.

<br>

  <li><strong>Launch a fully functional MVP (Minimum Viable Product):</strong><br>

<h2 align="center" id="setup">🛠️ Local Setup & Installation</h2>

To run <strong>Handyman Helper</strong> on your local machine, follow these steps:

<ul style="margin-left: 40px;">
  <li><strong>Clone the repository:</strong><br>
    &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;<code>git clone https://github.com/TGoncin/HandymanHelper.git</code>
  </li>
  <li><strong>Install dependencies:</strong><br>
    &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Navigate to the root directory and run <code>npm install</code>.
  </li>
  <li><strong>Environment Variables:</strong><br>
    &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Create a <code>.env</code> file in the root and add your <code>MONGO_URI</code> connection string.
  </li>
  <li><strong>Start the server:</strong><br>
    &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Run <code>node server.js</code> or <code>npm start</code> to launch the application.
  </li>
</ul>
<p align="center">
  <em>The marketplace will be available at <code>http://localhost:3000</code></em>
</p>
